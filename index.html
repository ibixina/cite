01 ABOUT

INVESTIGATING THE
COMPUTATIONAL MIND

Exploring how intelligent behavior emerges from computational processes, bridging neuroscience, AI, and cognitive psychology.

My research focuses on understanding fundamental principles underlying cognition—from perception and learning to decision-making and consciousness.

Through computational modeling and behavioral experiments, I investigate how complex mental phenomena arise from the interaction of simpler components.

I combine theoretical frameworks from dynamical systems, information theory, and machine learning to build models that capture cognitive richness.
